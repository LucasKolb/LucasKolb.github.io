ABOUT US

First conceived in August 2005 during Hurricane Katrina, Founder Kyle Ashish wanted to create an online arcade for people to play during the hurricane. However after a horrible fire hydrant accident, he was left in a coma for 17 years. Once he awoke he worked hard nights and days to fufill his hurricane dreams. Revitalized by the passion in his heart, the reflection in his soul, and the care on his mind, Dr. Ashish has developed the best online arcade platform since ROBLOX. Dr. Ashish now lives peacefuly in Mumbai, India, with his 32 children and his 3 cats and 2 dolphins.
